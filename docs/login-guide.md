# AMIS Login Guide

For AMIS students and parents using the Online Enrollment Portal.

## Option 1: Continue With Email

Step 1: Open the AMIS Enrollment login page.

Step 2: Enter your student or parent email address.

Example:

```text
student@gmail.com
parent@gmail.com
```

Step 3: Click **Continue with Email**.

Step 4: AMIS sends a secure verification link to your email.

Step 5: Open your inbox and click **Verify My Email**.

Step 6: After verification, AMIS opens your dashboard through that one-time secure link.

## Option 2: Continue With Google

Google sign-in is currently marked as coming soon. Use the email verification flow for now.
