# Requirements and Planning

## Goal
Build a clear online pre-enrollment portal for Al Munawwara Islamic School where applicants can register, complete enrollment forms, upload documents, submit payment proof, and track application status.

## Core Users
- Applicant or parent
- Admissions staff
- Finance staff
- Admin user

## Main Requirements
- Account registration and login
- Email verification
- Applicant dashboard
- Multi-step enrollment form
- Draft saving
- Document upload and review
- Enrollment fee payment submission
- Payment verification
- Application status tracking

## Planning Notes
- Keep the applicant journey simple: login, continue application, submit, pay, wait for review.
- Use clear status labels such as Draft, Submitted, Under Review, Approved, Rejected, and Payment Verified.
- Separate admission review from finance review.
- Keep mobile usability strong because many applicants may use phones.

## Open Decisions
- Final enrollment fee amount
- Final official payment account details
- Required documents per grade level
- Notification method after approval
- Admin roles and permissions
